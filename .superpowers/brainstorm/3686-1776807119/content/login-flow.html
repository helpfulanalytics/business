How should the login experience work?

This shapes the whole site structure. Where does a resident go when they click "Sign In"?

A

Modal Overlay on Landing Page

Clicking "Sign In" opens a glass-panel login form centered over the hero video. The video keeps playing behind it. One seamless experience — no page navigation.


Best for: Polished, app-like feel. The video stays alive and the page never reloads.
B

Dedicated Login Page

Clicking "Sign In" navigates to /login — a full-screen page with its own video background and centered login card. Clean separation of concerns.


Best for: Easier to deep-link, bookmark, and share. More government-portal conventional.
C

Inline on Landing Page (Split Layout)

The hero section itself has two columns — left side is the messaging, right side has the login form built directly into the page. No click needed to see the form.


Best for: Maximum conversion — login is always visible. Common for portals where login is the primary action.
