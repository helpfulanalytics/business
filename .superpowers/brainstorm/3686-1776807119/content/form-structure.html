How should the application form be structured?

A full government form has 15–20 fields. How should residents move through it?

A

Multi-Step Wizard

Form broken into 4 focused steps with a progress bar at the top. One section at a time — less overwhelming, fewer mistakes.


Step 1
Business Info
Step 2
Owner & Contact
Step 3
Legal & Tax
Step 4
Review & Submit
Best for: Reduced cognitive load. Users can save progress per step. Feels modern.
B

Single Long Page

All fields on one scrollable page, grouped into labeled sections. Residents can see the full scope upfront and fill at their own pace.


Business Information
Legal & Tax
Best for: Power users who want to fill everything at once. Simpler to build and validate.
